Library Management System

Welcome to LibS. This is a system that helps librarians, faculties, professors, and students for easier borrowing and managing books and to keep track of them.

Login Register

About

This Library Management System is designed for schools that have a library to manage it efficiently and effectively. It provides tools for tracking book borrowing, managing user accounts, and monitoring due dates.

Features

Book Borrowing and Returning

Users can borrow and return books easily through the system. Track all borrowed books in one place.

Accounts for Users and Admins

Separate accounts for students, faculty, and librarians. Each role has different access and permissions.

Due Dates for Borrowed Books

Automatic due date tracking for every borrowed book. Get notified before books are overdue.

Book Reservations

Reserve books that are currently unavailable. Get notified when your reserved book is ready for pickup.

Borrow History

View your complete borrowing history. Track all previously borrowed and returned books.

Search by Author or Genre

Find books quickly by searching through author names or book genres.
